Fair Play Policy

Our Commitment to Fair Play

FanLitePlay is committed to maintaining the highest standards of fairness, transparency, and integrity in all our contests and competitions. This Fair Play Policy outlines our commitment to ensuring a level playing field for all participants.

1. Equal Opportunity

All players have equal opportunity to participate in contests. We do not provide any player with unfair advantages or access to non-public information. Every player:

  • Has access to the same information about matches and players
  • Can select from the same pool of available players
  • Competes under the same rules and scoring system
  • Receives results based on actual match performance

2. Anti-Fraud Measures

We employ sophisticated technology and manual monitoring to detect and prevent fraudulent activities, including:

  • Multiple account creation and collusion
  • Suspicious betting patterns and activities
  • Unauthorized access and hacking attempts
  • Use of bots or automated systems
  • Match fixing or insider information usage

⚠️ Consequences of Fraud

Any player found engaging in fraudulent activities will face immediate account suspension, forfeiture of winnings, and potential legal action.

3. Transparent Scoring System

Our scoring system is based on actual match performance and is completely transparent:

  • All scoring rules are published and available to players
  • Points are calculated automatically based on verified match data
  • No manual adjustments are made to scores
  • Players can view detailed scoring breakdowns

4. Data Integrity

We ensure the integrity of all match data and player statistics:

  • Data is sourced from official cricket authorities
  • All information is verified before being used in contests
  • Regular audits are conducted to ensure accuracy
  • Any discrepancies are immediately corrected

5. Responsible Gaming

While FanLitePlay is completely free-to-play, we promote responsible gaming practices:

  • Age verification (18+ only) is mandatory
  • Players are encouraged to play for entertainment and skill development
  • We provide resources for responsible gaming
  • Players can set personal limits on their participation

6. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on FanLitePlay:

  • Creating multiple accounts to gain unfair advantage
  • Sharing account credentials with other players
  • Using automated tools or bots to play
  • Attempting to manipulate or hack the system
  • Colluding with other players to fix contests
  • Using non-public information to gain advantage
  • Abusive or harassing behavior toward other players
  • Violating any local laws or regulations

7. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes or concerns about fair play:

  • Players can file a complaint through our support system
  • All complaints are investigated thoroughly
  • Players are kept informed of investigation progress
  • Decisions are made based on evidence and policy
  • Players have the right to appeal decisions

8. Monitoring and Enforcement

FanLitePlay continuously monitors all activities to ensure fair play:

  • Real-time monitoring of all contests
  • Automated detection of suspicious patterns
  • Manual review by our compliance team
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Immediate action against violations

✅ Our Promise

We guarantee that FanLitePlay provides a fair, transparent, and secure environment for all players. Your trust is our most valuable asset, and we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity.

9. Updates to This Policy

FanLitePlay reserves the right to update this Fair Play Policy at any time. Players will be notified of significant changes. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
