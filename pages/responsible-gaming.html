Responsible Gaming

Our Commitment to Responsible Gaming

FanLitePlay is dedicated to promoting responsible gaming practices. While our platform is completely free-to-play with no real money involved, we believe it's important to encourage healthy gaming habits and provide resources for all players.

1. What is Responsible Gaming?

Responsible gaming means playing for entertainment within your means, understanding the risks, and maintaining control over your gaming habits. It's about enjoying games while keeping them in perspective as a form of entertainment.

2. Age Restrictions

FanLitePlay is restricted to users who are 18 years of age or older. We enforce strict age verification during registration to ensure compliance with this requirement. We do not allow minors to create accounts or participate in any contests.

3. Play for Entertainment

FanLitePlay should be enjoyed as a form of entertainment and skill development, not as a way to make money or solve financial problems. Remember:

  • Gaming should be fun and enjoyable
  • Never play with money you cannot afford to lose (though FanLitePlay is free)
  • Don't chase losses by playing excessively
  • Balance gaming with other activities
  • Maintain healthy relationships and responsibilities

4. Time Management

Healthy gaming includes managing the time you spend playing:

  • Set limits on how much time you spend playing
  • Take regular breaks during gaming sessions
  • Don't let gaming interfere with work, school, or relationships
  • Maintain a balance between gaming and other activities
  • Consider using our parental controls if you have family members who play

5. Warning Signs of Problem Gaming

If you notice any of these signs in yourself or others, it may be time to seek help:

  • Spending excessive time gaming
  • Neglecting work, school, or family responsibilities
  • Lying about gaming habits
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when not playing
  • Using gaming to escape problems or negative emotions
  • Continuing to play despite negative consequences
  • Unsuccessful attempts to reduce gaming

FanLitePlay provides tools to help you manage your gaming:

7. Resources for Help

If you or someone you know needs help with gaming habits, these resources are available:

  • National Council on Problem Gambling (US): 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537)
  • Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • Mental Health Services: Contact your local mental health provider
  • Family Support: Talk to a trusted family member or friend
  • FanLitePlay Support: support@fanliteplay.com - We're here to help

8. Parental Controls

If you're a parent or guardian concerned about gaming habits:

  • Monitor your child's online activities
  • Set time limits on device usage
  • Discuss healthy gaming habits with your child
  • Use parental control software if needed
  • Contact us if you have concerns about your child's account
