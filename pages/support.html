We aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible. Live chat typically responds within 5-10 minutes, email within 2-4 hours, and phone support during business hours.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email. You'll receive a password reset link within 5 minutes.

I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?

First, ensure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option. If you continue to have issues, contact our support team via live chat or email.

Can I change my account information?

Yes! You can update your profile information, email, and phone number in the account settings. Some information like date of birth may require verification for security purposes.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Please contact our support team with detailed information about the issue, including screenshots if possible. You can report bugs via email, live chat, or our bug report form on the website.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your information is never shared with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How can I delete my account?

You can request account deletion through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Do you offer technical support for app issues?

Yes! If you're experiencing technical issues with our website or app, our support team can help troubleshoot. Please provide details about your device, browser, and the specific issue you're facing.

What should I do if I encounter a payment issue?

Since FanLitePlay is completely free-to-play, there are no payment issues. However, if you have billing questions or concerns, contact our support team immediately.
